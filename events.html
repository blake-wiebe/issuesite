Events

Save The Blue

Save the Blue is a chairty event that raises money for teams that tavel the world cleaning ocean banks and helping protect native wildlife. The event itself is a one day event where those who come help clean the ocean, and after enjoy a free concert with food trucks.

Surfs Up

Surfs up is a fun competition to have fun and bring show the effects trash has on the ecosystem of the beach. The event has a grandprize for the surfer that shows off the best talent. The event will also have a host of food trucks at the beaches.

Run Under The Sun

Run under the sun is a triathlon event that shows those involved the true wonders of the ocean. The triathlon will be a fun course with water stations fruit stands and plenty of fun.

Paws in the Sand

A fun family friendly event with live music to raise money for ocean awareness. Bring your pet down to the beach to enjoy the nice sea breeze and ocean water.
